Birth

Caroline Wanjiru Mwangi was born on 6th April 1982 in Mararal, Samburu County. She is the first born child to Mr George Gilbert Mwangi and Mrs Gladys Muthoni Kamau. She is sister to the late Humphrey Gitonga.

Guciarwo


Mwendwoni-iri Carolyne Wanjiru Mwangi Maina aciarirwo tariki 6th April 1982 ituraini tia Mararal Samburu county ari mwana wa kirigithathi wa Gladys Muthoni Kamau na George Gilbert Mwangi. Ni mwari wa nyina na mutigairi Humphrey Gitonga.

Church

Carolyne was baptised at birth at the Allamano Catholic Church Maralal. She later attended liturgy classes, had her first communion and later confirmation according to her Catholic faith. She is a staunch christian and a prayer warrior.

Kanitha


Mwaka-in wa 1982, Carolyne niabatithiririo kanaithai-ni wa Allamano Catholic Church, Mararal. Niaigirire kirathi kanitha-ini na akiamukia giathi kia Mwathani kia mbere. Niathire na mbere na mathomo na akiheo umiriria.

Education

Caroline was enrolled at Saint Mary's Primary School in 1987 where she did her primary school studies and sat for her Kenya Certificate of Primary Education examination in 1995.The following year she joined Kisima Girls High School where she sat for her Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education exam in 1999. She then took a bridging course on soft computer skills at Janitus Computer Institute before joining Saint Paul University for a bachelors degree in Procurement and Supply Chain Management.

Githomo


Carolyne akinyia miaka ya guthii thukuru niaigirire thukuru wa St. Mary's Primary School mwaka-ni wa 1987 kuria ekire kigeranio gia KCPE mwaka wa 1995. Mwaka wa 1996 niangitire thukuru wa Kisima Girls High School na agithoma kinya mwaka wa 1999 kuria ekire kigeranio kia form four na akihetuka. Nia chokire agithomera kothi ya computer na akirikia. Agichokire akiigira St. Paul University na akigia degree ya Procurement and Supply chain Management na akiheo thumbi.

Employment

Caroline was employed by the Ministry of Roads and Public Works in 2006 as an Assistant Secretary. She left in 2012 and joined the Kenya Rural Roads Authority and was posted to Makueni County. Carolyne's last work station was in Kajiado County where she has been since 2019.

Wira


Kuri mwaka wa 2006, Carolyne niandikirwo kibarua ni Ministry ya Roads and Public works eta karani kuria arutire wira ngiya mwaka wa 2012. Niacokire akiadikwo ni KERRA (Kenya Rural Roads Authority) na agitomwo Makueni kinya mwaka wa 2019 riria aheirwo transfer na agitumwo Kajiado kuria ararutaga wira nginya kuhuruka gwake.

Marriage

Caroline met the love of her life Judge Maina Miano in 2006. The two fell deeply in love and before long formalised their courtship by marriage under Kikuyu Customary Laws.They have been blessed with two boys Henricoh Kamau Maina and John Maina Miano.

Kuhika


Mwaka-ini wa 2006, Carolyne niacemanire na mwadwa wake Judge Maina Miano na makihikania na mitugo ya Ugikuyu. Nimarathimirwo na ciana igiri Henricoh Kamau Maina na John Maina Miano. Carolyne niaredete muthuri wake na ciana ciake muno ona makiria ni aredete family yake.

Illness and Passing

Caroline fell ill on 4th of December 2021 and was treated at Kenol Hospital and discharged. Almost three weeks later on 19th of December 2021 her illness recurred and she was taken to Nairobi Womens Hospital - Kitengela and then transferred to Nairobi Womens Hospital - Ngong Road. Caroline rested on 20th of December 2021 while undergoing treatment at the ICU facility.She was 39 years old.

The family wishes to thank Doctors and the Health care workers at the Nairobi Womens Hospital for the care Caroline was accorded while at the hospital. We accept the will of the Lord in the belief that we shall meet with Caroline again. She has left behind her beloved husband Judge Maina Miano, co-wife Judy Wangui Maina and three children, Henricoh, Johnny and Ruby.

Ndwari


Kuri mweri inya wa December 2021, Carolyne nianjiririe guthinio ni mwiri na agitwaro thibitari wa Kenol na akirigitwo na akiheo rutha. Mweri 19 wa December 2021, niehiirwo ni dwari na akiguthokio Nairobi Womens Hospital-Kitengela agicoka agithamio Nairobi Womens Hospita-Ngong Road (ICU). Kuri mweri mirongo iri wa December, Carolyne niahurukirio ni Mwathani witu kuri matha-ini ma thaa igii cia hwaini. Ahuruka ari na ukuru wa miaka mirongo etatu na kenda (39). Ithue family nituracokeria arigitani othe a Nairobi Womens Hospital na othe area mateithenagia nao ni wira mwega mararutire na kugeria guteithia Carolyne. Atiga muthuri wake aredete na mwiru wake Judy Wangui na ciana ciao Henricoh,Johnny na Ruby.

Carolyne we loved you but God loved you most.
Rest in peace till we meet again dear.
Safiri Salama.
